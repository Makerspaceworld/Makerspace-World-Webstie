Makerspace is changing rapidly into Maker Education which focus on S.T.E.A.M

What is Maker or S.T.E.A.M education?

The makerspace or STEAM education is still in its early phase. This 'maker' movement is fundamentally changing the way we as educators and educational researchers envision teaching and learning. This movement focus on making - which is an active process of builing, designing and innovating with tools.

Why is S.T.E.A.M education important?

Everyday across the world people are using S.T.E.A.M in there daily lives or at their job. It is important that our students graduate with these skills and maintaining scientific and technological leadership which is essential to our development and our future. With the average age of the employees in the businesses being 50 or greater, there is more engineers/technicians and unkown number of professionals in the medical science, biotechnology, energy, clean tech, Robotics, IT etc. that needs to be replaced.
